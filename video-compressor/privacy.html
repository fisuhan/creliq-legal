Privacy Policy

Application: Video Compressor
Developer: Creliq
Product: Media Kit
Effective Date: July 20, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Creliq collects, uses, and protects information when you use Video Compressor.

1. About the Application

Video Compressor allows you to compress videos directly on your Android device. All video processing takes place locally on your device. Your videos are never uploaded to Creliq's servers for compression.

2. Information We Collect

The application does not require an account.

We do not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Phone number

If you contact our support team, we will receive the information you voluntarily provide in your email.

3. Media Files

Video Compressor only accesses media files that you explicitly choose.

  • Videos remain on your device.
  • Compression happens locally.
  • Videos are not uploaded to our servers.
  • We do not view or access your videos.

4. Permissions

The application may request permission to:

  • Read photos and videos you select.
  • Save compressed videos.

These permissions are used solely to provide the application's functionality.

5. Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the application is used and to improve performance and reliability.

Analytics may include:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Language
  • Usage statistics
  • Diagnostic information

6. Purchases

Premium subscriptions are managed through RevenueCat and Google Play Billing.

  • Payments are securely processed by Google Play.
  • Creliq never receives your payment card information.
  • RevenueCat processes limited purchase information required to manage subscriptions.

7. Local Storage

The application may store locally:

  • Compression history
  • Application settings
  • User preferences

This information remains only on your device.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Limited information may be processed by:

  • Google Firebase
  • RevenueCat
  • Google Play Billing

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the application. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children's Privacy

Video Compressor is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed toward children.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
