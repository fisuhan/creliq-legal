Data Deletion Policy

Application: Video Compressor
Developer: Creliq
Product: Media Kit
Effective Date: July 20, 2026

This Data Deletion Policy explains what data Video Compressor stores and how users can remove that data.

1. No User Accounts

Video Compressor does not require account registration or sign-in. We do not maintain user accounts or user profiles.

2. Media Files

All video compression is performed locally on your device.

  • Your videos are never uploaded to Creliq.
  • Your videos are never stored on our servers.
  • We cannot access your media files.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The application may store the following information locally:

  • Compression history
  • Application settings
  • User preferences

This information remains only on your device.

4. How to Delete Your Data

You may delete all locally stored application data at any time by:

  • Clearing the application's storage from your Android device settings.
  • Uninstalling Video Compressor.
Important:
Since Creliq does not store your media files or personal application data on its servers, deleting the app or clearing its storage permanently removes the application's locally stored data from your device.

5. Subscription Information

Premium subscriptions are managed by RevenueCat and Google Play Billing.

Subscription records required for billing and entitlement management are retained by those providers in accordance with their own policies.

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your Google Play account settings.

6. Analytics Data

Video Compressor uses Google Firebase Analytics to help improve the application.

Firebase may process anonymous or aggregated usage information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and data retention practices. Creliq does not use analytics information to identify individual users.

7. Third-Party Services

The application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • RevenueCat
  • Google Play Billing

Each service maintains its own privacy and data retention policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Deletion Policy from time to time. Any changes will become effective when published on this page.
